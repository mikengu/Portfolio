About me

Hello, I'm Mike. Welcome to my page. I'm a Web Developer currently living in Dallas, TX. I'm not sure when I became interested in coding, but it has always been a hobby for me, something I did for fun.

My background is in Biology and Healthcare. Becoming a developer has been a big transition but, surprisingly, the two fields complement each other well. I found that the problems I have while developing are similar to the ones I find in the lab, so by repeatedly applying the scientific method over and over again I can get to the solution.

My current project involes working on a team of developers and designers to help bridge the gap between those who need assistance and those who want to help. I have used Node.js and Express.js to setup the back-end scaffolding and routes, and MongoDB for data storage. Since then, I have moved to the front-end and am currently learning React.js to help set up the UI.
