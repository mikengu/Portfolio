About me

The thing that I enjoy the most is helping those that I can. In college I majored in Biological Science to gain a deeper understanding of how the human body work so that I can be better equipped to helping others. For me coding was a hobby, something I did for fun. It never occured to me that this hobby was something I can seriously pursue.

It wasn't until when I graduated and started working in the healthcare field that I realized how much I miss spending hours working on my simple programs. It took a few pushes along the way, but I finally decided to change my career path to being a developer

Since that day, I have not looked back. It is definitely not an easy field to be in, but I have not had a day where I am not enjoying what I am doing. Along the way, I have met many amazing people that have helped me immensely. I am sure that I would not have gotten to where I am now without their help, and I hope to provide help to others like they have for me.

Outside of pressing things on my keyboard, I have always enjoyed teaching. Recently I have been going to more local meetups/workshops to give mini presentations on technologies that I am familar with, and learning from more experienced developers. I'm also an avid bibliophile and gym junky, and I typically combine those two hobbies together. It is not uncommon for me to be lingering in the gym for too long because I get lost listening to an audiobook. Other than that, I can usually be found in the kitchen experimenting with something that I think will be delicious.
