About me

Hello, I'm Mike. Welcome to my page. I'm a Web Developer currently living in Dallas, TX. I'm not sure when I became interested in what I am currently doing, but it has always been a hobby, something that I did for fun.

My background is in Biology and Healthcare. It is a large transition from that to becoming a developer, but so far, the fields has been complementing each other really well. I have found that the problems I encounter while developing are very similar to the ones I encounter in the science lab. So the way I solve them is also the same, and that is to repeatedly apply the scientific method over and over again.

My current project involves working on a team of developers and desiginers to help bridge the cap between the people who needs assistance with those that can provide it. I have used Node.js and Express.js to setup the backend scaffold and routes, and MongoDB for data storage. Since then, I have moved onto the frontend and currently learning React to help set up the UI.

Whenever my mentor is not harping over me to write better code, I enjoy weightlifting and cooking. Luckily, those two things fit perfectly together. I am also a bibliophile, so I am constantly listening to one of my favorite podcasts or audiobooks whenever I can.
